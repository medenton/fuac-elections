Secretary Candidate Statements

ELIZA PIETT

"Let me preface by addressing the elephant in the room: Admittedly so, I know little to none regarding astronomy. Where I lack scientific understanding of the club content, I make up for it with an eagerness to be involved (and learn)! I believe I would prosper in this position, for I thrive in matters of organization and aesthetics. I would consider myself a highly social individual, with the creative ability to promote social interest. As secretary, I plan to engage with the Furman population by increasing the social media presence of the club. I intend to implement an “astronomy fact of the week” and similar captivating content. Through social media remodeling, I can confidently guarantee a boost in club engagement! Beyond social media maintenance, additionally, I would provide the club with high-quality visual media! Fun fact: My major is psychology, and I have a side passion for photography! While I have fallen short on attendance in the prior semester, I assure you that my presence would maintain consistency in the role of secretary!"
