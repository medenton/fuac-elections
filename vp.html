Vice Presidential Candidate Statements

KINJEE BHATT

"Hey guys my name is Kinjee, and I’m currently a member of the astronomy club here at Furman. As the president of this club, I hope to continue practicing some of my favorite parts of the club, such as starbeque, and planetarium shows. A few things I would introduce to the club would be visiting regional observatories, and perhaps introducing CLP’s that are hosted by specialists in astronomy-related fields. I think through having these events, we would be able to grow the club, and increase retention."

ELIZA PIETT

"I would be absolutely thrilled to fill the role of vice president of Astronomy Club! With this position, I would maintain strong, consistent communication with the president; assisting whatever needs come their way! Next year, my schedule is commitment-free (in regards to other club activity), so I would have more than enough time to maintain membership for Astronomy Club! As for the fulfillment of presidential duties, while I’m currently uneducated in the operation of the planetarium, I am a fast learner! Beyond the planetarium, I am reliable in matters of communication and scheduling, so presidency would be a position I'd confidently fill (in circumstances of absence/vacancy). As a sophomore, this position would be a beneficial learning experience. I assure you that I would not neglect the responsibility that comes with it!"
