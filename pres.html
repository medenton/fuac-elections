Presidential Candidate Statements

JOSH SWOPE

"Through my time as being Vice President for the past year in Astronomy, I have quickly learned all of what goes into the club, and more importantly what we can accomplish for the future of our club. From building community by hosting the Starbeque events each semester, to even taking several small groups to teach astrophotography both on and off campus, I have always had a passion for stars and for leadership. I was the president of the American Sign Language Club this past year, and through my leadership was able to increase regular participation from 2 members per meeting last year, to a steady, active, and excited 15+ members per meeting, as well as tripling our original budget through fundraising and campaigning in one semester. I have plans for the expansion of the Astronomy club such as increasing active membership, co-hosting more events with other organizations on campus, and creating more Astronomy events, all while still upholding the Astronomy Club’s beliefs and traditions. I plan to shoot for the moon with my passion for the stars, vote Josh Swope for Astronomy Club President."

KINJEE BHATT

"Hey guys my name is Kinjee, and I’m currently a member of the astronomy club here at Furman. As the president of this club, I hope to continue practicing some of my favorite parts of the club, such as starbeque, and planetarium shows. A few things I would introduce to the club would be visiting regional observatories, and perhaps introducing CLP’s that are hosted by specialists in astronomy-related fields. I think through having these events, we would be able to grow the club, and increase retention."
